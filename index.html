About Us

Established in the year 2014, at Pune, (Maharashtra, India), we, “LIMRA GAUGES AND TOOLS,” are engaged in manufacturing and supplying a wide range of high quality products such as Industrial Gauges, Jig and Fixtures, Carbide ball anvils etc. Our exclusive range includes Limra Ring Gauges, Limra Plug Gauges, Reversible Gauges, Snap Gauges, Pin Gauges, Limra Thread Gauges (Plug and Ring )–(OHNS AND CARBIDE )Machining Fixtures, Checking Fixtures, Taper mandrells,Taper Gauges, Die and Punches & Involute Spline Plug & Ring Gauges etc. Apart from this, we procure our range raw material from esteemed vendors of the industry.
